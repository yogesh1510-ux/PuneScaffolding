ABOUT THE COMPANY

A backbone of real estate industry, scaffolding is the unsung hero that ensures safety and smooth execution of any construction. Scaffolding has evolved from the use of wood and bamboo to sophisticated metal fittings, platforms and pipes. And with a vision to provide superior quality scaffolding that meets highest safety standards, Pune Scaffolding was incepted.

Among the most esteemed names of construction industry, Pune Scaffolding is now a respectable organization known for quality products and services. From undertaking turnkey projects to supply scaffolding equipment to consulting and assembling it on site, Pune Scaffolding does it all adheres to highest standards of safety, quality and service.
